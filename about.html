Vision

Bring yourself to Guru Nanak Institute Of Hotel Management




Guru Nanak Education Society, Nagpur came into existence in 2006. Guru Nanak Educational Society, Nagpur is headed by Sardar Navneet Singhji Tuli our beloved and revered CMD, a renowned politician and social worker who is dedicated to light the lamp of education in the society and has proven his excellence in doing so by setting up different technical and management study centers. GNIT & GNIET are some prominent ventures running successfully under his guidance . And now GNIHMCS is another feather added to GNI’s cap!

The institute has a sprawling lush green campus in the vicinity of MIDC, Kalmeshwar, with high-end, well equipped laboratories; well qualified, knowledgeable and experienced faculty members; Smart classrooms and a practical approach to education. Extra- curricular activities are encouraged to help students to exhibit and sharpen their various skills. The college is affiliated to Kavi Kulguru Kalidas Sanskrit University, Ramtek. It is our constant endeavor to ignite the inner passion of the students towards hospitality and sculpt them into world-class hospitality professionals.
