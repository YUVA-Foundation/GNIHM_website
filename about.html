About

Vision

To inculcate lifelong passion to serve and uplift rural,underdeveloped and urban communities alike by empowering the youth with education and unique skill sets.

Mission

To educate the masses, including minority students, economically weak students and students from rural areas with world class hospitality education and empower them into world class hospitality professionals.

Bring yourself to Guru Nanak Institute Of Hotel Management




Guru Nanak Education Society, Nagpur came into existence in 2006. Guru Nanak Educational Society, Nagpur is headed by Sardar Navneet Singhji Tuli our beloved and revered CMD, a renowned politician and social worker who is dedicated to light the lamp of education in the society and has proven his excellence in doing so by setting up different technical and management study centers. GNIT & GNIET are some prominent ventures running successfully under his guidance . And now GNIHMCS is another feather added to GNI’s cap!

The institute has a sprawling lush green campus in the vicinity of MIDC, Kalmeshwar, with high-end, well equipped laboratories; well qualified, knowledgeable and experienced faculty members; Smart classrooms and a practical approach to education. Extra- curricular activities are encouraged to help students to exhibit and sharpen their various skills. The college is affiliated to Kavi Kulguru Kalidas Sanskrit University, Ramtek. It is our constant endeavor to ignite the inner passion of the students towards hospitality and sculpt them into world-class hospitality professionals.


Principal Message


Welcome to the Guru Nanak Institute of Hotel Management and Catering Studies Family! GNIHMCS attracts students from all over India because of its highly equipped campus, training labs, eye for detail, focus on innovation and creative aptitude, and discipline. Our team at GNIHMCS provides quality education and empower our students to face challenges at a global level. Hospitality industry is growing by leaps and bounds all over and we at GNIHMCS would like to contribute to its growth story! There is a vacuum of trained hospitality graduates with courteous attitudes that we wish to fill. Thus we commit to provide employable and dependable professionals to the industry with our personalized and incessant guidance. It is our constant endeavour to keep our students abreast with the latest industry trends I thus welcome you to the fascinating world of hospitality and tourism. Come on board to join Guru Nanak Institute of Hotel Management and Catering Studies and become successful in life. For us hospitality isn't just a profession….it's our passion!
