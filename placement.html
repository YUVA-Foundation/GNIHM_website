Placement Officer Message

The world is your oyster when you graduate with us!

Hospitality industry is growing by leaps and bounds and we at GNIHMCS would like to contribute to its growth story! There is a vacuum of trained hospitality graduates with courteous attitude that we wish to fill. It is our constant endeavor to empower our students with the latest technical knowhow and soft skills to provide them with a cutting edge in the job market.
We at GNIHMCS realize that hospitality industry demands an individual who is technically sound and of brilliant demeanor. We understand that employability quotient of the student goes beyond the text books and hence we give ample focus on developing the right attitude and temperament in our students for the hospitality industry.
We prepare our students for campus recruitment by giving individual attention to their strengths and weaknesses. We empower our students with communication and employability skills and prepare them for Aptitude tests, Group discussions, Technical and HR interviews .We provide ample of opportunities for placements so students can identify their interests and aspirations and realize their dreams into a reality. We have strategic partnerships with international hotel brands and other hospitality organizations which offer a plethora of opportunities for our students.



Ms Smita Mardikar
T&P Officer
Welcome to the world of culinary brilliance!

We at GNIHMCS ignite and kindle the passion towards cooking and baking in our students and help them realize their dream of becoming a chef. As the students join us they are taught the basics of Indian and continental cooking along with basics of bakery and patisserie. As the students move on to the second year they are introduced to the colorful world of Indian regional cuisine. Students learn the tricks of the trade as they learn the precise science of quantity kitchen. As the students reach their final year they are taken on a global culinary journey. From French and Italian to Chinese and Thai, our students horn their skills as they develop their aesthetic. While the students gradually get their expertise on the world of culinary they embark on a parallel journey in the world of patisserie: from cupcakes, pastries, croissants, pies, cakes and much more.



Chef. Susan Vaz
